Here's what you need to know:

Communication is Key

We love to exceed your expectations, but first we need to know them! Communicate your expectations clearly to our office staff so that we may best meet your needs. If you have a particular task or objective, let us know! We're happy to help.

Our Products

We arrive at your home fully equipped and ready to go! We use electrolyzed water cleaning solutions and disinfectants; we do not use harsh chemicals. Maid Brigade’s Exclusive PUREcleaning System uses allergy-free and anti-bacterial color-coded microfiber cloths and mops. Our HEPA-filtration vacuums are also certified through the Carpet and Rug Institutes Green Label program.

NOTE: PUREmist® disinfection is available upon request and is not automatically included in every service. Please let us know ahead of time if you would like it added.

Our Teams

Our cleaning teams are employees with competitive pay and benefits, not independent contractors. This means we can guarantee every person we send into your home for integrity and quality. It also means our staff is fully insured, including being covered by Worker’s Compensation insurance, so there is zero risk or liability on your end.

How to prepare for your appointment:

Cancellation / Reschedule

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment for any reason, please call or email us at least 48 business hours prior to your scheduled cleaning date to avoid the late cancellation fee.

De-Clutter

You know where things go in your home better than we do! Our teams are best when focusing on cleaning tasks, so it’s best that you handle clutter and putting things away before we arrive.

Pets

If you have any pets, please let us know so we can make our teams aware. Dogs should be securely put away or outside while we service your home. We do not clean any pet waste.

Arrival Times

We will arrive between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM. To get a two-hour window, please call or email our office as early as 7:30 AM on the morning of your service.

Entry

The easiest method is our Key Service. We’ll keep your key coded and secure between visits. You can also provide a garage code or hide-a-key location.

What's Included in Your Service

The Basics (Included in Every Service)

General

  • Remove cobwebs/dust bunnies
  • Dust all baseboards, windowsills, door frames, furniture, wall hangings, decor, blinds/shutters, ceiling fans, light fixtures, heating units & vents
  • Take out trash and replace can liners when provided
  • Make all beds
  • Clean all countertops, sinks, showers, tubs, toilets, and mirrors
  • Clean the exteriors of all appliances and cabinets and the inside of microwave
  • Clean inside of window over sink (if applicable)
  • Clean interior of sliding glass doors (one set)
  • Vacuum and mop all floors

Disinfection

  • Use PUREmist® to disinfect all high-touch surfaces, including door knobs, light switches, entire bathrooms, etc. (available upon request)

Deep Cleaning (The Basics + the items below)

  • Full & deep dusting of all surfaces, furniture, wall hangings & decor, fans & light fixtures
  • Hand-wiping of all baseboards, windowsills, door frames, light switches, blinds/shutters, heating units & vents
  • Clean inside all empty cabinets and drawers (upon request)
